Ekashunyam 3.0

The Dark Tech Carnival

Ekashunyam 3.0 is a State-Level inter-collegiate IT fest organized by the Department of Computer Science at SDM College.

Scheduled for 02 April 2026, the event will be held at the Indraprastha Auditorium within the campus of SDM College, Ujire.

Ekashunyam 3.0 aims to create an engaging platform where participants can challenge their problem-solving skills, explore new ideas, and interact with fellow tech enthusiasts.

This year’s fest embraces a unique theme inspired by the mysterious and iconic world of Wednesday, bringing a dark, quirky, and intellectually stimulating atmosphere to the event.

The theme reflects curiosity, individuality, and unconventional thinking—encouraging participants to push boundaries and approach technology with creativity and originality.

More than just a competition, Ekashunyam 3.0 is a celebration of innovation, learning, and collaboration.

It provides students with opportunities to test their skills, gain new experiences, and connect with like-minded individuals who share a passion for technology.

Step into the shadows of innovation and experience the techno-mystery at Ekashunyam 3.0.

“Dare to enter… else Stay wondering what you almost discovered.”
