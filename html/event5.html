📍 Seminar Hall I

📜 Rules

  • Team participation (2 members per team)
  • Participants are expected to be in formal attire during the event.
  • Present an innovative IT-related product or idea
  • Presentation time: 5–10 minutes (including demo)
  • PPTs and prototypes are allowed
  • Participants must bring all necessary equipment required
  • Idea must be original
  • Teams must answer judges’ questions
  • Judges’ decision will be final

💻 Product Specifications

  • Must fall under domains like Web/Mobile, AI/ML, IoT, Cybersecurity, Cloud, Data Science, Blockchain, or AR/VR

⚙️ Technical Expectations

  • Clear system architecture or workflow
  • Use of modern technologies/frameworks is encouraged
  • Prototype / wireframe / working model adds weight
  • Should show scalability and real-world use

🌍 Problem-Solving Focus

  • Must solve a real-world problem
  • Clearly define target users, problem, and solution
  • Preference for social impact & practical usability

📄 Documentation Requirement

  • Each team must prepare a clear and structured report
  • Include idea overview, problem analysis, solution design, and tech stack
  • Provide system workflow or architecture explanation
  • Explain feasibility and real-world impact of the product
  • Report must be presented if requested during evaluation

🚫 Restrictions

  • No purely theoretical ideas without structure
  • No direct copies of existing products
  • Modified ideas must show clear innovation
